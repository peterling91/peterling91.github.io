What is the Professional Competency Model (PCM)? How is it different from the typical way of learning?

LET’S FIND OUT ~~

Typically, subjects are taught to learners one at a time, each subject separate from the others...

Learners like yourself have to combine the relevant knowledge and skills, and apply them to real-world settings... we know that's difficult!

Here at Nanyang Polytechnic, we teach differently to make sure that you are ready for work and life!

Let us show you how our unique Professional Competency Model (PCM) will transform your learning.

First, we extract the relevant knowledge and skills from each subject that help you to solve real-world problems.

Next, we combine them with real-world knowledge and skills needed to help you excel in the workplace.

Brewing in progress... Your learning units are being created.

A WHILE LATER ---

TADA-! Now you have a Competency Unit!

These are contextualised learning units that combine relevant knowledge and skills from different subjects, which can include...

Curricula co-developed with major industry players, training co-certified by leading companies and classes co-taught by industry experts.

That’s not all! You get to apply your knowledge and skills in projects and internships/clinical attachments.

These are also known as Work-Integration Units (WIUs)!

WIUs are learning units that mirror work situations to help you integrate your knowledge and skills from the relevant Competency Units, so you can tackle real-world challenges!

Through the PCM, you will take multiple Competency Units and Work-Integration Units.

These learning units help you further your education and succeed in the workplace.

What is a Work-Integration Unit?

Work-Integration Units (WIUs) are tasks that mirror work situations, which can be in the form of projects or internships/clinical attachments.

WIUs are learning units that help to integrate your knowledge and skills from the relevant Competency Units, so you can tackle real-world challenges.

Through the PCM, you will take multiple Competency Units and Work-Integration Units which help you further your education and succeed in the workplace.
