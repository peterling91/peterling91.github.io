Experiences

Student Life Academy

We believe that learning should be flexible and accessible, so we have created a wide range of innovative programmes and workshops that allow our students to develop essential life skills at their own pace and time.

Lunch Time Talks

Fuel your mind with inspiring talks by industry professionals, staff, alumni, and student leaders. They will enhance your critical thinking and provide you fresh perspectives!

Life Skills Workshops

From design thinking to conflict management, our hands-on workshops can help you turn your newfound knowledge into practical advice that you can apply in everyday life.

Learn-to-Play Series

Unleash the athlete, musician, or linguist within you through exciting adventures with our certified coaches! Dive into a new world of excitement for a nominal fee.

Projects-By-Students-For-Students

Unleash your passion project and be the driving force behind engaging events and workshops.

Grow through CCAs

NYP also offers more than 80 co-curricular activities (CCAs) for students to pursue their passions, expand their network and pick up new hobbies.
