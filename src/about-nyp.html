About NYP

Established in 1992, Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) offers quality education and training through 40 full-time diploma courses and a wide range of continuing education and training (CET) options.

Our Mission

Empowering learners for work & life Co-creating with industry for growth & sustainability

Our Vision

The Innovative Polytechnic A nexus of future-ready people, learners & industry

Our Values

Nurturing & Caring Integrity Can-Do Spirit Innovative & Enterprising Teamwork

Our strategic goals

Nurturing future-ready learners

  • Competency-based learning
  • Lifeskills as competitive advantage
  • Ownership of learning

Co-creating value with industry

  • Strategic industry collaboration
  • NYP360 value propagation

Committing to sustainability

  • NYP as a living lab for environment sustainability

Engaging & empowering our people

  • Staff engagement
  • Ownership of capability development

Living our innovation & enterprising culture

  • Organisation transformation
  • The Innovative Polytechnic brand
