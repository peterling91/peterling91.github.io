Skills for Good Festival 2023

Career workshop

Organised by Central Singapore CDC

28 & 29 July 2023, Friday & Saturday
10 am - 8 pm
Bishan Junction 8 Atrium (Level 2)
Register now

Presented by Central Singapore Community Development Council, the ‘Skills For Good Festival 2023’ aims to promote skills for the good of Self, Community and for Life. This year’s edition will help Singaporeans learn more about industry trends, jobs and skills in Singapore’s key growth areas – the Green, Digital and Care economies.

Key highlights

  • Sign up for Learning Journeys to a sustainable crab farm, Maritime Drone Estate and more!*
  • Try your hands at simple repair of household items
  • Learn caregiving tips for bedridden patients
  • Explore courses in the Green, Digital and Care economies
  • Get complimentary consultation with Career Coaches and Skills Ambassadors
  • Participate in stage games/ demonstrations and stand to win exciting prizes

*Pre-registration required

NYP will be putting up two showcases as well as a performance by students from K-Wave CCA on 28 July!
