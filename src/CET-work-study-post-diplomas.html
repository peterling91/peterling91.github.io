Work-Study Post-Diplomas

For early career individuals to gain a career headstart

Explore how this innovative initiative empowers students to gain practical skills, and secure a brighter future.

Applicants need to be shortlisted and interviewed by participating employer(s).

Successful candidates will be offered a job with a salary and a $5,000 sign-on incentive.

Participants will work full-time for the company and also study part-time as part of the WSP.

Upon successful completion of the programme, you gain a certification, diploma or post-diploma qualification.

Eligibility requirements

Polytechnic graduates

The WSP programme is specially designed for recent graduates of polytechnics who are in the transition from school to workplace.

Early career individuals

Applicants must be within 36 months of graduation. For those who need to serve National Service, the 36-month period will start from ORD.

Singapore Citizens / PR

Only Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents are eligible to apply.
