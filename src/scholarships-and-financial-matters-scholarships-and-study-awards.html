Being a full-time NYP student comes with great perks, including a variety of scholarships you are instantly eligible for. Offered by NYP and our partners, these scholarships recognise students with outstanding academic achievements, leadership qualities and active CCA participation.

Some scholarships continue to be available for you in Year 2 and Year 3.

Available scholarships for you

The scholarship list may change from time to time. For further enquiries, email scholarships@nyp.edu.sg
