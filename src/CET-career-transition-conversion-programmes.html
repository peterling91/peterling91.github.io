Career Transition / Conversion Programmes

Transition smoothly into a new domain of your choice

Explore how this innovative initiative empowers students to gain practical skills, and secure a brighter future.

Choose from industry-relevant courses in sectors with good hiring opportunities.

SCTP programmes have modular training courses of 3 to 12 months duration with part-time and full-time options available.

Receive advisory prior to course enrolment to help you select suitable courses for you.

Get employment facilitation services and career advisory to enhance your employment prospects.

Available programmes

Diploma programmes are suitable for those with O level, ITE, WSQ certs or similar experience

Skillsfuture Career Transition Programme (SCTP)

A train-and-place programme, where participants are assisted in their training to help with their job search.

Duration 3 to 12 months

Career Conversion Programme (CCP)

Individuals are usually hired by a participating employer before undergoing training to take on a new job role.
