Admissions

Early Admissions Exercise (EAE)

For who

If you have a strong aptitude and interest in a particular field, you can apply through the EAE, which grants you a conditional offer into your diploma of choice prior to your O-level final examinations.

Application period

Opens around June every year

Learn more about EAE

Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE)

For who

After receiving your O-level result, you can apply though the annual JAE organised by MOE based on your academic merit.

Application period

12 January - 17 January 2024

Learn more about JAE

Direct Admissions Exercise (DAE)

For who

If you are ineligible under JAE and EAE, please to apply via DAE for admission to full-time diploma courses.

Application period

1 January - 31 March 2024

Learn more about DAE
