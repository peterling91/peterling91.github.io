In FY23, Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) continued to advance its commitment to sustainability through a series of impactful initiatives and strategic collaborations. This report highlights our key achievements and outlines our ongoing efforts to integrate sustainable practices across all facets of our operations.

Key Highlights

  • Reduced Energy Consumption: Implemented advanced energy management systems, resulting in a 10% reduction in energy usage across the campus.
  • Renewable Energy Integration: Expanded our solar panel installations, now powering 25% of our campus with renewable energy.
  • Retrofitting Projects: Upgraded existing buildings with green technologies, improving overall energy efficiency.

Looking ahead

As we move forward, NYP remains dedicated to enhancing our sustainability efforts. Our future goals include achieving carbon neutrality, further integrating sustainability into our curriculum, and expanding our community outreach programs. We are committed to fostering a culture of sustainability, empowering our students, staff, and partners to contribute to a greener future.
