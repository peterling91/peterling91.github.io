Applying to DAE (A Level)

Skip to content
  • Direct Admissions Exercise (DAE)
    • Applying to DAE
    • Portfolio requirements

DAE is an annual application process conducted by Ministry of Education to allow holders of Singapore-Cambridge A Level certificates to seek admission to our array of dynamic diploma courses, providing a direct pathway from A Levels to specialized polytechnic education.

Can I apply?

Apply for your desired full-time diploma courses in NYP through the Admission Exercises conducted for A Level holders.

  • You may use your O Level results or equivalent (e.g. school-based results) to apply for the 3-year diploma courses.
  • Meeting the minimum entry requirements (MER) is not a guarantee that you will be shortlisted. Shortlisting is based on merit and course availability in the year of application.
  • NYP reserves the right to amend the information provided in this webpage without prior notice.

Some courses require an additional portfolio for direct admissions:

  • For courses under School of Design & Media, you are required to prepare a digital portfolio as part of the application process
  • For some courses under School of Business Management, you will be contacted to submit a portfolio if you are shortlisted
Check portfolio requirements

If you are shortlisted for the Oral Health Therapy course, you are required to take a manual dexterity test.

Entry requirements

You may refer to the list below for the entry requirements for selected 2-year or 2.5-year courses.

How do I apply?

Step 1

Apply online

Application period: 7 Aug to 18 Aug 2023

Applicants for 2-year and 2.5-year courses starting in Semester 2 (October Intake) can submit their applications online by creating an account and following the steps provided in the DAE application form.

After submitting the online application, you will need to upload the following via our Document Submission System within 3 working days from the date of your application submission (refer to the instructions in the Online Application System):

  • Identification document (front & back if applicable) or passport
  • Result slip or academic transcript
  • CCA records
  • Digital portfolio (if applying for courses offered by School of Design & Media)
  • Company Sponsorship Form (PDF, 0.1MB) (if applicable)

Please ensure your name / identification number is clearly shown in all the documents submitted.

If you encounter any problems, please reach us at 6451 5115 during operating hours or contactNYP@nyp.edu.sg. Please provide your name and contact details so that we can assist you better.

Apply online now
Step 2

Application outcome

Application outcome will be released by end March/April or end September/October. You may login to the online application system listed above to check your application status.

Successful applicants will receive enrolment notifications via sms and email by end March/April or end September/October.
