Jobs-Skills Integrator (JSIT)

Skip to content
  • Specialist centre

  • Jobs-Skills Integrator (JSIT)

The Jobs-Skills Integrator (JSIT) initiative was announced by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance, Mr Lawrence Wong, on 14 Feb 2023.As the JSIT for the Precision Engineering (PE) sector, Nanyang Polytechnic will:

  • Engage companies to understand their manpower and skills needs and review existing training programmes to meet the needs of the industry.
  • Partner the Singapore Precision Engineering and Technology Association, SPETA, to engage companies in the association's network.
  • Work with employment agencies and other training providers to enhance placement support and training for companies in the sector.

About Precision Engineering (PE)

The Precision Engineering (PE) industry is the backbone of the manufacturing industry in Singapore.

According to statistics from the Singapore Economic Development Board (EDB), over 2,700 PE firms supply products and expertise needed to manufacture complex components and equipment used in industries such as semiconductors, medical technology, marine, offshore and aerospace.

The refreshed PE Industry Transformation Map (ITM) 2025 also highlighted that the industry is expected to achieve a manufacturing value-added growth of 3.2 percent annually to S$15.27 billion by 2025. The ITM effort will also create 3,200 new PMET jobs by 2025.

About Centre for Digital & Precision Engineering (CDPE)

The Centre for Digital & Precision Engineering (CDPE) at NYP is the hub for precision engineering activities. It supports training for precision engineering students and the developmental and technological needs of the local Precision Engineering (PE) industry. The centre has also established strategic partnerships with key global leading companies in the precision engineering process development and equipment manufacturing areas, allowing the centre to remain technologically relevant and adopt the best industry practices in precision engineering worldwide.

TOver the years, the centre has established strategic partnerships with leading companies in the precision engineering sector both locally and globally. CDPE continues to work closely with industry partners to help grow the precision engineering sector.
