    The Tertiary Tuition Fee Subsidy (TTFS) Scheme is administered by Yayasan Mendaki. It is an education subsidy for Malay students which covers their Tuition Fees.

    • Please refer to Yayasan Mendaki website for more details.
    • Please call Yayasan Mendaki @ 6551 2840 or email for more enquiries.

    Can I apply?

    To apply you must meet the following requirements:

    • This scheme is not applicable to Poly Foundation Programme students.
    • You are a full-time subsidised diploma Malay students who are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents with at least one Singaporean citizen parent.
    • Your race or the first component of the double-barrelled race is Malay.
    • Your monthly household per capita income (PCI) does not exceed $2,000.

    Subsidy value

    Per Capita Income (PCI) Eligible Subsidy
    S$ 1,400 and below 100%
    S$1,401 to S$1,700 75%
    S$1,701 to S$2,000 50%

    How do I apply?

    Apply online

    • Applicants can submit their applications online via Online application via http://tfas.mendaki.org.sg/
    • Hardcopy application is not allowed.

    Application outcome

    • Successful applicant will be notified directly by Yayasan Mendaki.

    Notes:

    For Mendaki, there is only one application deadline for each semester, i.e. 30 April for Semester 1 and 31 October for Semester 2. If student submits an application in Semester 1 but beyond Semester 1's application deadline, the application is deemed to be meant for Semester 2.

  • Application period

    • Semester 1 - by 30 April
    • Semester 2 - by 20 December

    Can I apply?

    • For Singaporeans only, who have balances in their PSEA. To check balances in the PSEA, call MOE PSEA Hotline at 6260-0777.
    • For JPAE (ITE) applicant : If you have earlier establised a standing order (SO) for use of Post Secondary Education Account (PSEA) under ITE, your SO instructions also apply for all future course fees withdrawals in NYP until the SO is terminated or replaced.

    Subsidy value

    • Up to 100% of Tuition Fees and Supplementary Fees, subject to available balance in PSEA.

    How do I apply?

    Apply online using own PSEA

    • If you are 21 years old and above, login via PSEA Online application System using own Singpass Account.
    • If you are below 21 years old, please request your parent/legal guardian to login via PSEA Online application System with his/her Singpass Account to submit the PSEA Standing Order application on your behalf. A legal guardian is required to upload the relevant legal document (e.g. Court document) to validate that he/she is your legal guardian.

    • Semester 1 - by 30 April
    • Semester 2 - by 20 December

    Can I apply?

    • For all full-time subsidised diploma students.

    Loan details

    This is a loan scheme administered by CPF Board. For more information, please refer to CPF Website.

    • Loan amount
      • Up to 100 % of Tuition Fee, subject to the available balance in their CPF savings under the education scheme. The scheme does not cover supplementary fees.
    • Interest payable
      • Prevailing CPF Interest rate. Interest will be charged from the time the savings are withdrawn.
    • Repayment period and amount
      • Maximum of 12 years, minimum of $100 per month.
    • Commencement of loan repayment
      • 1 year after graduation or 1 year from the date of leaving the course, whichever is earlier.

    How do I apply?

    Singapore Citizens and Singapore Permanent Residents

    • Online application by using SingPass to login at CPF Website and proceed to My Request -> Education / Dependents' Education -> Apply to use CPF for my education.
    • Hardcopy applications are not allowed.
    • All parties to the application, i.e. student and CPF member must have a SingPass, 2-Step Verification (2FA) and email account before application can be made online.
    Apply online now

    International Students

    • Complete the form, eSign and email the softcopy to NYP_Finance@nyp.edu.sg.

    Notes:

    • Check Application Schedule to use CPF Savings under the CPF Education Loan Scheme.
    • For CPF, there is only one application due date for each semester, i.e. 30 April for Semester 1 and 21 December for Semester 2.
    • CPF Board does not accept late application beyond the application due date for each semester. If student submits an application in Semester 1 but beyond Semester 1's application due date, the application is deemed to be meant for Semester 2.

  • To ensure that no students is denied access to Polytechnic education because of financial difficulties, MOE, through Nanyang Polytechnic has made available the Tuition Fee Loan. DBS Bank is the agent bank appointed to administer the Scheme.

    Can I apply?

    • For all full-time subsidised diploma students.
    • This scheme is not applicable to Poly Foundation Programme students.

    Loan details

    This is a loan scheme administered by CPF Board. For more information, please refer to CPF Website.

    • Loan amount
      • Up to a maximum amount of 75% of Tuition Fees. The scheme does not cover supplementary fees.
    • Interest payable
      • The average prime rate of the 3 local banks, i.e. DBS, OCBC and UOB. Interest will be charged from 1st June or 1st December in the year of graduation.
    • Repayment period and amount
      • Maximum of 10 years, minimum of $100 per month.
    • Commencement of loan repayment
      • 1 year after graduation or 1 year from the date of leaving the course, whichever is earlier.
    • Criteria for guarantor
      • From 21 years old to 60 years old;
      • Singapore Citizen - for Singapore Citizen applicant;
      • Singapore Citizen / Singapore Permanent Resident - for Singapore Permanent Resident applicant;
      • Singapore Citizen / Singapore Permanent Resident - for foreigner applicant; and
      • Not an undischarged bankrupt.
