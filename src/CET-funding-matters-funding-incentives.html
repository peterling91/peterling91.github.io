Course fee subsidies

To encourage more Singaporeans to continue lifelong education and training, there are generous subsidies (up to 90%) for Singapore Citizens and PR.

Please see the actual course information for the exact subsidies. Find a course now.
For more details on SSG’s funding mechanism.

SkillsFuture credits

Singapore citizens and PR can also use their SkillsFuture credits for most of our courses. Please see the actual course information to check if that course accepts SkillsFuture credits as a payment mode.

Study loans

Adult Learners of NYP CET Full Qualifications can apply loans from the bank (such as POSB or OCBC) at their respective website or branches.

Please note that these loans are provided directly to the students by the bank and is a commercial loan agreement between the student and the bank.

These are subject to each bank’s loan policy, the bank’s credit scoring of the student/applicant, and their respective terms and conditions.

It is advisable for learners to carefully review all terms and conditions of each bank’s loans before signing up.

NYP has no influence and interest in these loan applications.

Refund policy

Refund details may vary based on course and withdrawal timing. Refer to specific course information for precise refund information.

Other discounts and schemes

  • All NYP Alumni who intend to enrol in selected NYP's Public Short courses will enjoy a 10% discount on the course fee.
