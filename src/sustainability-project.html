Innovative and Sustainable Architectural Design

As part of NYP’s commitment to sustainability and energy efficiency, we have undertaken a significant upgrade of our centralised chiller plant. This project focuses on optimizing our campus cooling system, reducing energy consumption, and minimizing our environmental footprint.

Highlights

Collaboration and Innovation

Advanced technology

Implementation of high-efficiency chillers equipped with the latest variable speed drive technology to enhance performance and reduce energy usage.

Smart Controls

Integration of smart control systems that adjust cooling output based on real-time demand, ensuring optimal energy use and consistent indoor comfort.

Green Refrigerants

Use of environmentally friendly refrigerants with lower global warming potential (GWP), contributing to a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions.

Sustainability Benefits

Collaboration and Innovation

The Centralised Chiller Plant Upgrade project is a testament to NYP’s collaborative approach to sustainability. We have worked closely with industry partners, leveraging their expertise and innovative solutions to achieve our sustainability goals. This project also provides valuable learning opportunities for our students and staff, allowing them to engage with real-world sustainability challenges and solutions.

At NYP, we are dedicated to creating a sustainable campus that sets a benchmark for others to follow. The Centralised Chiller Plant Upgrade is just one of the many initiatives we are undertaking to advance our sustainability agenda and create a greener future.
