Modular arrangement

A modular arrangement is applicable to some post-diploma programmes. Instead of committing 18 continuous months to complete the programme, you have can take the prescribed modules in 6-month courses, earning a Post-Diploma Certificate upon completion of each 6-month course. If you obtain all 3 Post-Diploma Certificates within 3 years, you will be conferred a post-diploma full qualification.
