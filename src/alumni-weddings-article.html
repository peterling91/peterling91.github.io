Liang Chen & Miguel

Published on 29 July 2022

Congratulations, Liang Chen and Miguel!

Liang Chen and Miguel met in 2012 during the Direct Poly Admission (DPA) orientation at Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP). They started dating while pursuing their diplomas offered by different schools in NYP. Liang Chen was from the School of Health Sciences (renamed School of Health and Social Sciences) while Miguel was from the School of Information Technology. Their relationship continued after graduation as they became working professionals. After nine years, they finally sealed their vows and got married in 2021. We wish the couple a blissful marriage!
