Beyond NYP

After graduating from NYP, you can...

Enrol in prestigious universities

Many of our graduates have pursued prestigious degree programs at both local and international universities that offer advanced standing and credit transfers for admissions into their degree programmes.

  • Nanyang Technological University (NTU)
  • National University of Singapore (NUS)
  • Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT)
  • Singapore Management University (SMU)
  • Singapore University of Social Sciences (SUSS)
  • Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)
  • University of the Arts Singapore (UAS)

Have your dream job or be your own boss

Many of our graduates have gone on to work at top companies or blazed their own trails as an entrepreneur. NYP offers career guidance for our students, and have many initiatives to support students in their innovative and enterprising ventures.
