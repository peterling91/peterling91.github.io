Education & Research

Education & Research

All Year One and Two students are introduced to compulsory General Studies Modules (GSM), covering a total of 30 hours. Of these, 13 hours are dedicated to sustainability content. These modules include specific case studies in Singapore and within NYP.

Students also take sustainability-related modules within their diplomas or do inter-disciplinary projects across Schools. When students do their Final Year Projects – which are projects done in collaboration with the industry – about 50% of such projects have a sustainability focus, or elements to reflect on sustainability.

We strive to be a catalyst for good. And we can help you do the same.
