Academic transcript & certificate

Skip to content
  • Academic transcript & certificate
  • OpenCerts

Read below for information on how to request and collect the various academic documents that you may need after graduation.

  • Eligibility

    You can request a copy of your academic transcript anytime during your course of study and after you have graduated. The fee for each copy of transcript is S$5.35. There will be no refund of payment once you have submitted your request.

    How to request and collect?

    You may request your academic transcript via the following methods:

    1. i@Central

      • You can make your request personally or authorise another person to do so on your behalf through an authorisation letter.
      • The letter should include the authorised person’s name and identity card number. The person must produce the letter and his/her identity card for verification.
      • You or the authorised person can collect the academic transcript at the i@Central on the day of request.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours and modes of payment.

    2. Student Portal

      • If you are a current student or have left NYP recently (i.e. within six months) you can make your request via the Student Portal.
      • Payment must be made via eNETS Debit for internet banking account holders with DBS Bank, POSBank, UOB or CitiBank N.A.
      • NYP will send the transcript to the mailing address indicated in your online request form, within seven (7) working days of the request.

    3. AXS

      • You may request via the AXS stations by selecting Education > Nanyang Polytechnic to make your request for academic transcript and make the payment.
      • You or the authorised person can collect the replacement certificate at the i@Central physically and the AXS transaction receipt must be produced during collection.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours and modes of payment.
  • Eligibility

    You must be a recent graduate within six months of conferment of qualification.

    How to request and collect?

    You may request for the letter of certification via the following methods:

    1. Student Portal

      • Make your request online at the Student Portal.
      • The letter will be mailed to the mailing address, given in the request form, within seven (7) working days of the request.

    2. i@Central

      • For requests beyond six months of graduation, kindly contact i@Central.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours.
  • How to collect?

    You may collect your certificate via the following methods:

    1. Graduation Ceremony

      • The graduation certificates would be issued at the Graduation Ceremony.

    2. i@Central

      • If you did not attend the Graduation Ceremony, you can collect your certificate at the i@Central.
      • You can collect the certificate personally or authorise another person to do so on your behalf through an authorisation letter.
      • The letter should include the authorised person’s name and identity card number. The person must produce the letter and his/her identity card for verification.
      • You or the authorised person can collect the certificate at the i@Central after seven (7) working days from the last day of the Graduation Ceremony.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours.

    Notes:

    Please note that the Polytechnic will not be responsible for the loss or damage of any uncollected certificates.

  • Eligibility

    If you are a graduate, you can request a replacement copy of your graduation certificate. The fee for each copy of the replacement certificate is $21.40. There will be no refund of payment once you have submitted your request.

    How to request and collect?

    You may request for a replacement of your graduation certificate via the following methods:

    1. i@Central

      • You can make your request personally or authorise another person to do so on your behalf through an authorisation letter.
      • The letter should include the authorised person’s name and identity card number. The person must produce the letter and his/her identity card for verification.
      • You or the authorised person can collect the certificate at the i@Central, four (4) working days after the day of request.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours and modes of payment.

    2. AXS

      • You may request via the AXS stations by selecting Education > Nanyang Polytechnic to make your request for replacement of graduation certificate and make the payment.
      • You or the authorised person can collect the replacement certificate at the i@Central physically and the AXS transaction receipt must be produced during collection.
      • Please click here for the i@Central’s operating hours and modes of payment.
